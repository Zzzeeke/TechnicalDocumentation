Introduction

Genshin Impact is a free-to-play open world action-adventure RPG where you can play a vague and mysterious "Traveler" from another world who arrives in the land of Teyvat. After trying to go to an another adventure together with your twin, you were blocked by an Unknown God who'm you and your twin try to defeat it. But as misfortune occurs your twin got captured, and you were been sealed for over 500 years. After waking up on a beach you set off on a quest to find your lost twin and gaining your original powers by absorbing the 7 elements of the world.

The 7 seven elements of the world that you can absorb and use in battle are as follows: Anemo which means Wind, Geo which means Earth, Electro which mean Lightning, Dendro which means Plants/Trees, Hydro which means Water, Pyro which is Fire, and lastly Cryo which means Ice.

  • Prologue

    You set foot on the Land of Freedom which is associated by the Anemo element. In this land the people and it's local god believe that you must be free as the wind. Thus the god of Anemo granted its people Freedom, thus the Crown of the North is not under any rulership, but what does Freedom truly means if Demanded to by a God.

  • Chapter 1

    As you continue your journey to find your twin you get in the Land of Contracts/Commerce. Also known as the heart of whole Teyvat because the currency that all people of this world is created by the Geo Archon by its divine power. But what could even a god do if he too could not escape the inevitable death. In the end he will sign the contract to end all contract.

  • Chapter 2

    After being a hero in the Land of Commerce you arrive at the Nation of Eternity. In this nation of Thunder and Lightning their local deity seeks only Eternity traumatized by her past grief after loosing her sister in the Archon war, for she belives that Eternity are you closest to the Heavenly Principles. For that she restricts anyone from entering and exiting her nation in order to protect them from divine judgement greater than her. But what do mortals see of the eternity chased after by their god, thus retaliating to be free from her strict grasp and show her that a nation can move on even not violating the Heavenly Principles.

  • Chapter 3

    After you opened the heart and mind of the god of thunder you continue to seek answers behind your twin's location your next objective is to go and adventure on the Land of Wisdom. In the land of wisdom there is a push for folly, yet the god of Wisdom makes no argument against it. After entering the City of Scholars you quickly learned the dark secrets kept by the nation who's governance is centralized by an Academy. Rejecting their own god yet creating another god which is the pinnacle of human wisdom. For the God of Wisdom's enemy is wisdom itself thus as granting you the title of The First sage and the god's permission it is in your hands to give divine punishment for those who oposed their god.

Wishing System

There are three types of Wishing System: Limited-Event Caharacter Banner, Limited-Event Weapon Banner, and Permanent Banner. You can win a 50% chance on these banners, loosing the 50% on the limited time events gives you a random 5 stars character or weapon. When you lost the 50-50 chance you will get the character/weapon you want on your next set of wishes. There is also a pity or a counter system which counts how many you wished so far. If you wished for 75th time your 50-50 chance begins.

The limited-event character wish banner features newly released characters of the game or returning characters. Grab the chance to wish on them while you still can. The same implies to the limited-event weapon wish banner features the limited-event character's best in slot weapons. Which can empower them more and make your team stronger against the enemies.

The permanent event wish banner has no time limit, as the name suggests it is permanent. You could 5 stars character and weapons. The one you could lose on the limited-event wish banners are also available here with only 1% of 12 odds.

Weapons

Weapons are only obtainable through limited-event wish banner, chests, and events.

There are 5 types of weapon throughout the game which is Claymore, Sword, Bow, Catalyst, and Polearm. A Claymore is a heavy big sword which mostly weilded by Adult type of characters. A Sword is a lightweight version of a claymore characters weilding this weapon are mostly swift and agile moving. A Bow are those who prefer to kill enemies from distance it requires an amazing aiming ability. A Catalyst are those who prefer to play as a mage in the game, they carry grimoire as a weapon. Lastly a Polearm or a Spear, people who prefer a long weapon to use in battle.

Artifacts

Artifacts are relics to boost your character's power and make them even more stronger. There is a RNG (random number generator) on obtaining stats on your artifacts. You could receive Atk%, Def%, Hp%, or elemental % artifacts these rng drop rates can be both a blessing or a curse to your characters making them either strong or stagnant.

Specific Artifacts for Specific Elements are sealed through domains. For example Dendro element characters prefer the Deepwood Memories artifact series because it bossts their damage and overall power.

Enemy Types

There are 3 types of enemies you can encounter through the vast world of Teyvat.

  • Weekly Boss

    These are the limited time boss which respawns every week on Monday 5am. You can only defeat them once every week. For a maximum 3 boss per week.

  • World Boss

    These boss are scattered around the map. You can challenge and defeat them countless time to your hearts content. But it requires 40 fragile resins in exchange for their drops

  • Mobs

    Mobs are the most common enemies in every game they are all over the map and can easily be defeat.

Housing System

For those who want some Sims 4 vibe of gaming Genshin also provides that type of game with their very own Housing System obtained after finishing a quest. The housing system is inside a magical teapot similar to a genie's lamp. But of course you need to collect materials outside your teapot.

Consequently, you can earn in-game currencies inside of your teapot by building a custom blueprint for your characters. They can give your gems in exchange for your hard work building their prefered envireonment of living.
